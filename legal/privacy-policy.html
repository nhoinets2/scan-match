Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Summary: Scan & Match collects your account information, wardrobe photos, style preferences, and subscription data to help you make better shopping decisions. We use AI to analyze your photos and RevenueCat to process subscriptions. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Introduction

Scan & Match ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using Scan & Match, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Data Type Examples Purpose

  • Anonymous customer identifier

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Services: Analyze clothing items, match against your wardrobe, generate outfit suggestions
  • Personalization: Tailor recommendations based on your style preferences and wardrobe
  • Account Management: Create and manage your account, authenticate users
  • Subscription Management: Process subscriptions, manage access to Pro features, track free tier usage limits
  • Communication: Send password resets, account notifications, subscription confirmations, and service updates
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with the App to improve features
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

4. AI and Image Processing

Scan & Match uses artificial intelligence (powered by OpenAI) to analyze clothing photos. When you scan an item or add wardrobe photos:

  • Images are sent to OpenAI's servers for analysis
  • AI extracts information such as clothing category, colors, style attributes, and fit characteristics
  • OpenAI processes images according to their Privacy Policy
  • We do not use your images to train AI models

5.1 Service Providers

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and in-app purchase processing
  • Apple/Google: Social authentication and payment processing (if you choose to use it)

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

5.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's infrastructure. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Access controls and user-specific data isolation
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Wardrobe Items: Retained until you delete them or your account
  • Scan History: Retained until you delete individual checks or your account
  • Subscription Data: Retained for the duration of your subscription plus any legally required period for financial records
  • Usage Counts: Retained to track lifetime usage against free tier limits
  • Analytics Data: Retained in aggregated, anonymized form

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as maintaining financial records).
