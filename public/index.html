Features

Everything you need for smarter shopping

From scanning items in-store to organizing your entire wardrobe, Scan & Match helps you build a cohesive, stylish collection.

Instant Scan

Point your camera at any clothing item in-store or from your gallery to instantly analyze its colors, style, and compatibility with your wardrobe.

Digital Wardrobe

Build your complete digital wardrobe with photos of everything you own. Categorize by type, color, and occasion for easy matching.

AI-Powered Matching

Our advanced AI analyzes colors, patterns, and styles to give you accurate compatibility scores and personalized outfit suggestions.

Match Results

Get detailed compatibility scores showing exactly which items in your wardrobe work best with your potential purchase.

Style Preferences

Set your style preferences – casual, minimal, office, sporty, and more – to get recommendations tailored to your personal aesthetic.

Secure & Private

Your wardrobe photos and style preferences are securely stored. We never sell your data or use your images to train AI models.
